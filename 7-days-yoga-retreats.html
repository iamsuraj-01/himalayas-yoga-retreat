7 Days Yoga Retreat

Experience the ultimate journey of self-discovery and transformation with our 7 Days Yoga Retreat at Himalayan Yoga Retreats. This week-long retreat is perfect for those looking to fully immerse themselves in the practice of yoga, meditation, and mindful living. Set against the serene backdrop of Rishikesh and the majestic Himalayan foothills, this retreat offers a comprehensive program designed to rejuvenate your body, mind, and spirit.

Overview

Discover the holistic approach of our 7 Days Yoga Retreat, designed to deepen your understanding and practice of yoga in a tranquil environment. Each day is structured to provide a balanced mix of physical asanas, meditation, and spiritual teachings, ensuring a transformative experience for all participants.

Eligibility

Our retreat is open to everyone, regardless of experience. Whether you are a beginner or an experienced practitioner, we welcome all who wish to deepen their practice and connect with like-minded individuals.

You can enroll in the retreat if you:

  • Aged between 18 years to 75 years
  • Children, families, students and/or backpacker
  • No prior yoga asana or meditation experience is required
  • Planning to spend some time in nature and connect to your inner self
